Admission Procedure



"Admission open for classes Pre KG to Class IX. For further details,you are requested to contact school office from 8:00 am - 3:00 pm."

The following documents are required to be submitted at the time of registration for admission.

  1. Registration form complete in all aspects.
  2. Parents' Profile.
  3. Xerox of the final Marksheet/Progress Report of the class last attended. (Not for PlayGroup and L.K.G.)
  4. 3 Passport size photographs.
  5. Xerox of 'Sent-Up' results of Class X (for class XI only).

The registration forms will be screened and incomplete forms will be summarily rejected. Eligible candidates will be submitted to an Entrance Test and Interview and those who qualify will be offered admission strictly on the basis of merit and the number of seats available. Those who do not turn up for admission by due date will forfeit ttheir claim and admission will be offered to those in the waiting list.
The following documents are required to be submitted at the time of admission:

  1. Transfer Certificate from the previous school in original. (Not for Playgroup, L.K.G. and U.K.G.)
  2. Date of Birth Certificate.
  3. Original Marksheet/Progress Report of the class last attended in the previous school.
  4. Transfer Certificate from the previous school in original.
  5. (Not for Playgroup, L.K.G. and U.K.G.)
  6. Date of Birth Certificate.
  7. Original Marksheet/Progress Report of the class last attended in the previous school.

Under very special circumstances the Principal may allow a candidate a grace period of maximum 2 weeks to submit the above documents. However, if the candidate fails to furnish them within the time allowed he/she shall have no claim to continue in the school and his/her name will be taken off the rolls without any compensation.
